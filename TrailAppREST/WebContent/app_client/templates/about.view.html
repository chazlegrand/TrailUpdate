Trail Update

Trail Update was created in 2017 as an effort to crowd source information about trail conditions, giving users the ability to search, filter, and update information. While mantained by a small team of dedicated developers, it is dependent on you, the user, to stay current. Enjoy the site and please, submit reports often! Happy trails!
